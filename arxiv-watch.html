★ CLIR: Liveness-Driven and Structure-Aware Fuzzing for the Cranelift Compiler

  • Track: WASM
  • Authors: Shangtong Cao, Tianlei Song, Qiuping Yi, Tianyu Chen, Guoai Xu, Ningyu He, Haoyu Wang
  • arXiv: 2606.26977v1
  • Published: 2026-06-25
